UNDERCOVER

The Ultimate Party Word Game

🎭
Blend In or Stand Out?
Civilians have a word. Undercovers have a similar one. Mr. White has nothing. Can you tell who's who?
v2.0 · Made with ❤️ for party nights

How to Play

01

Setup

Choose number of players and enter their names. Set the number of undercovers and whether to include Mr. White.

02

Secret Roles

Pass the phone. Each player taps to secretly view their word. Civilians share one word; Undercovers get a similar but different one; Mr. White sees nothing.

03

Discussion

Everyone gives one clue about their word without saying it directly. Be vague enough to hide your role, specific enough to prove you're not Mr. White.

04

Vote & Eliminate

Vote to eliminate the most suspicious player. Eliminated players reveal their role. Civilians win if all undercovers are out. Undercovers win if they match civilian count. Mr. White can guess the civilian word to win!
