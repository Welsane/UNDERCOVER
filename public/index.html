How to Play

01

Create or Join

One player creates a room and shares the 4-letter code. Everyone joins from their own phone — no Wi-Fi sharing needed!

02

Secret Roles

Each player privately sees their role. Civilians get a word. Undercovers get a similar but different word. Mr. White gets nothing.

03

Describe

Everyone submits ONE clue — type it or record a voice note! Be clever: too obvious and undercovers blend in; too vague and you'll look suspicious.

04

Listen & Vote

Play everyone's voice notes on your phone. Then vote to eliminate the most suspicious player!
